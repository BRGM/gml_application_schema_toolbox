  • GeoSciML WFS from Sweden: http://resource.sgu.se/service/bedrock1m_gsml_gu/wfs?service=WFS&amp;request=GetCapabilities

  • GeoSciML WFS from Finland: http://gtkdata.gtk.fi:8081/geoserver/wfs?service=WFS&amp;version=2.0.0&amp;request=GetCapabilities
